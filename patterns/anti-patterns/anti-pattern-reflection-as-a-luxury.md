# Anti-Pattern: Reflection as a Luxury

#### Description

Reflection and improvement are deferred until “things slow down.”

#### Why It Emerges

* Urgency dominates
* Improvement is not owned
* Pausing feels risky

#### Consequences

* Repeated mistakes
* Burnout
* Fragile speed
* Shallow learning

#### Signal

> “We’ll improve after this release.”
