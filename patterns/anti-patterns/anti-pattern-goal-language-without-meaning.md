# Anti-Pattern: Goal Language Without Meaning

#### Description

Goals are framed as outcomes but describe activities, outputs, or vague aspirations.

#### Why It Emerges

* Desire to appear outcome-focused
* Discomfort with measurable behaviour
* Fear of accountability

#### Consequences

* Direction without clarity
* Metrics that don’t guide learning
* Weak strategy–execution linkage

#### Signal

> “Improve customer satisfaction.”
