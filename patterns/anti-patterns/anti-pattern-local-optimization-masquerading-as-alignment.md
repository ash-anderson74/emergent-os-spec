# Anti-Pattern: Local Optimization Masquerading as Alignment

#### Description

Teams optimize locally while system-level outcomes degrade.

#### Why It Emerges

* Limited visibility
* Output-based incentives
* Fragmented strategy communication

#### Consequences

* Duplication
* Conflicting priorities
* Strategic drift

#### Signal

> “Every team is doing the right thing — somehow the system isn’t.”
