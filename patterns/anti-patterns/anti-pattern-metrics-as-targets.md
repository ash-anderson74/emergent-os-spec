# Anti-Pattern: Metrics as Targets

#### Description

Coaching language is used, but decisions and authority remain centralized.

#### Why It Emerges

* Coaching is rebranded management
* Leaders lack coaching skills
* Trust is conditional

#### Consequences

* Cynicism
* Reduced autonomy
* Learned helplessness

#### Signal

> “We empower teams — within these very strict rules.”
