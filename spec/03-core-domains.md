## Domain Relationships

These domains are interdependent and cyclic rather than hierarchical:

![core-domain-relationships](.gitbook/assets/core-domain-relationships.png)

Each domain feeds the next:

* Purpose defines intent
* Leadership shapes the environment for teams
* Product teams create adaptive value
* Flow enables responsiveness
* Learning feeds insight back into strategy and culture

Across these domains, EmergentOS emphasises a few universal principles:

* Empowerment over control
* Outcomes over outputs
* Learning over certainty
* Trust over compliance
* Flow over friction
* Systems thinking over silos
